DAY 3: Light Rail Transit Authority-Line 2 and National Museum Of the Philippines

Published by:

Fred Anthony D. Yu

Published on:

July 6, 2024

Introduction

On the third day of our company visit, we explored the Light Rail Transit Authority (LRTA) Line 2. Our tour began at the maintenance facility, where Sir Adrian Saldana explained the LRT's operations and detailed their repair and maintenance procedures. We then visited the control room, observing how they monitor CCTV feeds from all stations, manage train routes, and communicate with drivers to ensure smooth operations. Lastlly of our visit, we experienced a ride on the train, gaining firsthand insight into the LRT system's vital role in daily commuting.

In the afternoon, we visited the National Museum of Fine Arts. We walked through the museum's galleries and saw many pieces of Philippine art. One highlight was seeing works by famous artists like Juan Luna. His well-known painting, the Spoliarium, really stood out. The Spoliarium shows fallen gladiators being dragged away, and it made a big impression on us. Our visit to the museum was a great experience. It helped us understand and appreciate the rich history and beauty of Philippine art. This visit was a wonderful way to end our day, leaving us thoughtful and inspired.
